
# 📱 QuoteIt

Daily quotes & random quotes by tag – share as beautiful images.

---

## 📖 Description

Discover inspiration every day with **QuoteIt**! Whether you need a quick motivation boost or want to explore quotes by topic, QuoteIt has you covered. Share your favorite quotes as beautiful images with friends or on social media.

---

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

- Android Studio
- Kotlin
- Jetpack Compose
- libs.dev.shreyaspatil.capturable

---

## ✨ Features

- **Tag-Based Browsing**  
  Browse quotes by categories like motivation, love, life, friendship, and more.

- **Today’s Quote**  
  Start your day with a fresh, inspiring quote every day.

- **Share as Image**  
  Turn any quote into a beautiful image and share on WhatsApp, Instagram, or any social platform.

- **Simple & Clean Interface**  
  Focus on quotes without distractions.

- **Offline Access**  
  View quotes even without internet connectivity.

---

## 🌟 Why Choose QuoteIt?

- Boost your daily motivation and positivity.
- Explore quotes on your favorite topics.
- Share inspiration with friends and family in seconds.
- Safe for all ages (13+); no personal data collected.

---

## 🚀 How to Run

1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/srprawinraja/QuoteIt.git
2. Open the project in **Android Studio**.
3. Sync Gradle files.
4. Connect an Android device or start an emulator.
5.  Click **Run ▶️** to launch the app.

## ▶️ Usage

1.  Open the QuoteIt app.

2.  Browse quotes using category buttons.

3.  Tap on a button to generate a quote.

4.  Use the navigation buttons below the quote to:

    -   Save your favorite quotes

    -   Share quotes as images

5.  Share your inspiration on social media or with friends.
